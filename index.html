History of Takoyaki-Wahnsinn

Takoyaki Wahnsinn was founded on October 15, 2024 with a vision to bring an exciting and flavorful twist to the beloved Japanese street food. What started as a single shop in Kawit, Cavite quickly gained popularity leading to the opening of two more branches in Dasmariñas and Imus.

The brand is built on a fusion of flavors inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Japan, Germany and the Philippines. With every bite customers can taste the passion and expertise gained from these cultures creating a takoyaki experience like no other.

With a strong commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction, Takoyaki Wahnsinn is on a mission to expand nationwide and become the most recognized and beloved takoyaki brand in the country. This is just the beginning soon, everyone in the Philippines will know the taste of true takoyaki madness!
